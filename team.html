Academic & translational partners
μCytoD™ is being evaluated in collaboration with academic labs and translational groups working on immunotherapy, gene editing and nanoparticle delivery.
Immunotherapy group (demo)
Running pilot studies on CAR-T and NK cell engineering using μCytoD™ for non-viral delivery of mRNA and CRISPR RNPs.
Gene editing consortium (demo)
Benchmarking editing outcomes and off-target profiles in multiple cell types, comparing μCytoD™ with leading electroporation systems.
Nanomedicine lab (demo)
Exploring nanoparticle-mediated drug delivery using mechanoporation to access otherwise refractory intracellular targets.
